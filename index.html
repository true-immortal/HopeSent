HopeSent is an AI sentiment analysis web app leveraging Azure services to screen for mental health risks. It addresses challenges in mental health screening such as accessibility, result interpretation, and guidance. The app offers self-identification through validated questionnaires like PHQ-9 and GAD-7, with Azure Cognitive Services and Machine Learning providing risk assessments and personalized feedback. Immediate guidance on lifestyle changes, professional help, and resources is also provided, making it user-friendly for both the public and frontline health workers.

Services

Sentiment Analysis

The Sentiment Analysis Feature in our web app evaluates user emotions through voice recordings or text, determining sentiment scores and positivity/negativity levels. This aids in timely support and risk mitigation for mental health issues like anxiety and PTSD.

QuickCheck Mental Health Screening

Utilizes PHQ-9, GAD-7, PHQ-15, and Anxiety Attack Questionnaires for rapid assessment. Provides instant, concise results to gauge depression, anxiety, and somatic symptoms. A quick and effective tool for early mental health evaluation.

Screening
